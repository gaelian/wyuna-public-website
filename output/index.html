Welcome!

The Wyuna Community Inc is a charity organisation located in the Australian Capital Territory. Our focus is on sustainable living research and exploring alternatives to wage dependance.

The services we provide to members include:

Subsidised housing

Mental health counselling and support

Career advice and life skills training

Conflict resolution training

Wyuna Community Inc is a charity registered with the Australian Charities and Not-for-profits Commission.
